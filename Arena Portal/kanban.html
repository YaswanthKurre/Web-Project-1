Kanban Methodology in Project

  1. Initiating the Project and Setting Goals

    • Kanban Board Setup: At the project's outset, a comprehensive meeting was held to brainstorm ideas and establish a project roadmap with defined deadlines.
    • Task Planning: Various project aspects, including the decision to develop a Casino interface, were discussed and mapped out.

  2. Designing and Refinement

    • Framework Design: The project commenced with crafting the website's foundational structure using Canva.com.
    • Tracking Progress: Progress in design and iteratively refining the website's appearance was tracked and managed, ensuring alignment with objectives.
    • Continuous Enhancement: Employing the Kanban methodology encouraged ongoing enhancements as the team collaborated to optimize the website's aesthetics and user experience.

  3. Efficient Project Management and Progress Tracking

    • Progress Monitoring: Utilizing Kanban, the team efficiently tracked individual team members' progress and ensured everyone stayed on track.
    • Adaptability and Flexibility: Kanban's flexible nature allowed for seamless navigation of the design process whenever necessary, contributing to an improved website.
    • Meeting Deadlines: The systematic application of Kanban enabled the team to successfully meet all project deadlines, demonstrating the efficacy of this methodology.
